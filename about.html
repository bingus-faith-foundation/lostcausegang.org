Lost Cause Gang emerged from Lost Voice, an electronic hip-hop duo founded in 2018 by artist producer Yung North and producer alexwenthome. Duo grew into an independent five-person collective in less than a year. Due to disagreements among the collective's members, it never produced any results and fell apart later that year with only its founding members remaining, who eventually changed their stage names to Sever.Ami and alxwnth.

Lost Cause Gang is a fully inependent entity that operates entirely remotely with its homeland being the World Wide Web. Our mission is to empower emerging artists in various genres to grow and be heard.
